DSP56001 
(DSP56001RC20, DSP56001RC27,XSP56001RC33, DSP56001FE20, DSP56001FE27, 
XSP56001FE33, DSP56001FC20, DSP56001FC27, XSP56001FC33,  DSP56001FD20, 
DSP56001FD27, XSP56001FD33)
Fixed-point, 24-bit general-purpose DSP featuring 512 words of full-
speed, on-chip program RAM, two 256-word data RAMs, and on-chip 
bootstrap hardware.  20.5 MHz, 27 MHz, or 33 MHz available for sale 
in PGA, CQFP or PQFP packaging.

DSP96002 
(XC96002RC33, XC96002RC40)
Dual-port, IEEE floating-point, programmable digital signal processor 
available in 33 MHz or 40 MHz in PGA packaging.

<more>      DSP56156 
(XC56156FE40)
High-speed 16-bit core processor combined with many intelligent 
periperals, 8k bytes of on-chip memory.  Ideal for voice-based 
applications.  Available third quarter.

DSP56ADC16 
(XC56ADC16S)
Single-chip, sigma-delta linear, 16-bit, oversampling, analog to digital 
converter, providing output sample rates of 100 kHz at 16 bits or 
400 kHz at 12 bits.

DSP56200 
(XC56200LC10)
A 28-pin HCMOS, algorithm specific, DSP peripheral designed to do 
cascadable FIR filtering or adaptive FIR filtering.

DSP56ADC16EVB
Evaluation board for DSP56ADC16.

DSP56000ADSx
DSP96000ADSx
<more>      Application development systems for designing, debugging, and 
evaluating DSP56156, DSP56000 and DSP56001, or DSP96002 target 
system equipment.  Available for IBM PCt, Sun3t, and Macintosh IIt.

DSP56KEMULTRCABL
Emulator cable adds full, real-time emulation capability to the 
DSP56000ADS.

DSP56156CLASx
DSP56000CLASx
DSP96000CLASx
Development software for DSP56156, DSP56000 and DSP56001, or 
DSP96002 processors includes a relocatable assembler, callable 
simulator modules, a linker, and a simulator which supports 
simulating multiple-DSP or single-DSP systems.  For IBM PC, Sun3, 
Sun4, NeXT and Mac II.

DSP56KCCx
Full Kernighan and Ritchie C compiler for development of DSP56000 
family applications.  For IBM PC, Sun3, Sun4, and Mac II.

DSP320to56001
<more>      Translator software converts 32010 .lod files into DSP56001 code.  
Source code provided.
